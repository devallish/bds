# British Deer Society Mobile App — Ideas Backlog

*A holding space for ideas raised during scoping that sit outside the confirmed technical design. Nothing here is committed scope — see [BDS_Mobile_App_Technical_Design_Overview.md](BDS_Mobile_App_Technical_Design_Overview.md) for what's actually been decided. Entries are dated so it's clear when each idea was raised, and can be promoted into the main design doc once BDS decides one is worth pursuing.*

## Ideas

### Member training & qualifications record (2026-07-12)
BDS delivers training and qualifications through external providers. Idea: show these on a member's account within the app — which courses/qualifications they hold, when obtained, and expiry/renewal dates where applicable.

Open question: would this need to integrate with each external provider's system, or would it be self-reported/admin-entered by BDS staff?

### Digital qualification card / wallet pass (2026-07-12)
An external-facing card showing a member's qualifications and activities — potentially issued as an Apple Wallet / Google Wallet pass rather than a plastic card. Lantra (the UK land-based skills awarding body) already does something similar for their certifications and is worth looking at as a reference model.

Depends on the training/qualifications record above existing first.

### Deer identification guide (2026-07-12)
An in-app reference guide for identifying deer species — a natural companion to the existing observation/survey forms.

### Health check checklists (2026-07-12)
Structured checklists for carrying out deer health checks (e.g. post-cull inspection). Could plausibly reuse the same "field record" data structure already described in the main design doc for surveys, observations, and stalking reports, rather than being a separate system.

## Notes

None of the above is scoped or prioritised — they're captured here so they aren't lost, not to design them prematurely.
